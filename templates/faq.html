Frequently Asked Questions



  • How can I trust you?
    Well, you can't really. We can promise that we are deleting every single note after its viewed, but we cannot prove that. You can verify yourself that the app uses end-to-end encrpytion by inspecting the client side JavaScript.
  • I don't want to set a password.
    You don't have to! Whilst its strongly recommended to set a password, its fully optional. You can leave the password blank if you don't want to set a password.
  • Are my notes secure?
    Yes. We use a military-grade encryption algorithm. The encryption is done in the client-side, if you want to you can access the code to verify it yourself.
  • Do you track or sell my personal information?
    No. We can't even access your notes in plaintext.
